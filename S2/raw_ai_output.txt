Gap ID: G1
Classification: G1 Missing Requirements
Description: Requirement for item inspection before refund approval is not reflected in the requirements specification.
Evidence:

T3: “Returned items must be inspected before refund approval.”
AC4: “A returned item must be inspected before refund approval.”
Requirements specification lacks any requirement mandating inspection prior to refund approval.
Impacted Artifacts: T3, AC4, R1–R14
Severity: High
